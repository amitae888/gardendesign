About Our Company

Garden Decor Consultants is a premier garden design and outdoor decor company serving homeowners across the United States.

Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate landscape architects and garden enthusiasts, we've helped thousands of clients transform their outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional environments that reflect their personal style and enhance their quality of life.

Our approach combines artistic vision with practical knowledge of plants, materials, and regional climate considerations to create gardens that are not only stunning but also sustainable and low-maintenance.

Our Design Process

How we bring your garden vision to life

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your vision, needs, preferences, and budget. This includes a site visit to assess your outdoor space's unique characteristics.

2

Concept Development

Based on our consultation, we create preliminary design concepts that include layout plans, material selections, and plant recommendations tailored to your specific region and climate zone.

3

Design Refinement

We present our concepts and collaborate with you to refine the design based on your feedback. This iterative process ensures the final plan perfectly aligns with your vision.

4

Implementation

Once the design is finalized, we provide detailed plans and can recommend trusted landscape contractors for installation, or work directly with your preferred professionals to ensure the design is executed properly.
