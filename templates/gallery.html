Project Spotlight

Featured transformation: Seattle Urban Retreat

The Challenge

Our clients had a small, sloped backyard in Seattle's Queen Anne neighborhood that was virtually unusable due to poor drainage, lack of privacy, and limited flat space. They wanted:

  • A functional outdoor living area for entertaining
  • Privacy from neighboring properties
  • Low-maintenance plantings suitable for partial shade
  • A solution to the drainage issues
  • Year-round visual interest

Our Solution

We created a multi-level garden with terraced areas to address the slope while maximizing usable space:

  • Installed a permeable paver patio with built-in seating
  • Created a rain garden to handle drainage issues
  • Planted a mixed screen of evergreens and deciduous trees for privacy
  • Incorporated native plants that thrive in Pacific Northwest conditions
  • Added subtle landscape lighting for evening enjoyment
