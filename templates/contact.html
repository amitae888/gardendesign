Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our consultation process

After you submit your contact form, one of our garden design consultants will reach out to you within 1-2 business days to discuss your project needs and schedule an initial consultation. We'll answer any immediate questions you have and provide information about our process and pricing.

Our initial on-site consultations are $150, which includes a 60-90 minute property evaluation, professional design recommendations, and preliminary budget planning. This fee is applied as a credit toward your design services if you decide to move forward with our company.

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer this option or are located outside our service areas. Virtual consultations are conducted via video call and include a review of photos or videos of your space that you provide in advance. Virtual consultations are $100 and also apply toward future design services.

To make the most of your consultation, it's helpful to prepare:
  • Photos of your current outdoor space
  • Examples or inspiration images of styles you like
  • A rough idea of your budget range
  • Information about how you want to use the space
  • Any property surveys or plans you may have
Don't worry if you don't have all of this information - we can help you figure things out during the consultation.
